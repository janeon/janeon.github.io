I am currently a fifth year doctoral student at CMU S3D advised by Professor Haiyi Zhu from the Human Computer Interaction Institute.

I research ways of alleviating impacts of algorithmically managed work using design, empirical, and systems-building methods. Specifically, I design and build systems that engage with multiple stakeholder groups, so as to develop policy and technology innovations that engender more equitable, empowered, and sustainable futures of platform-based labor.
